Architecture

This architecture is described at a high level only. It intentionally avoids operational details.

Conceptual layers

Ontology
Terms, entities, and meaning boundaries.
Interpretive
Rules that frame interpretation and reduce ambiguity.
Constraintive
Hard constraints (scope, safety, legality, provenance).
Authority
Who can assert, certify, or publish canonical truth for a domain.

Core roles

  • Publisher (publishes doctrine)
  • Operator (executes audits and governance under mandate)
  • Verifier (checks conformance claims)
