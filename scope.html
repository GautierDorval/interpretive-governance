Scope

normative Doc ID: IG-DOC-SCOPE · Doctrine 0.2.0 · Non-operational

Scope boundary: This site describes concepts and intent. It does not provide an audit method that can be copied and executed.

Explicit exclusions

  • Any test suite, dataset, or rubric intended to be run as-is
  • Any operational playbook for incident response or client delivery
  • Any “how-to deploy” instructions for production environments

What you can expect

Definitions, conceptual architecture, and a stable vocabulary designed to support professional discussions and future standardization, without exposing proprietary execution.
