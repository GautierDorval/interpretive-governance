Output constraints

normative Doc ID: IG-DOC-OUTPUT-CONSTRAINTS · Doctrine 0.3.0 · Non-operational

Even when a response is legitimate, not every output form is permitted.

Declared output boundaries

Public role
Doctrine, boundaries, definitions, and refusal conditions are in-scope.
Out of scope
Calibrated scoring, implementation steps, deployment instructions, and operational mandates are excluded.
Confidence
Confidence must track declared support, not fluency.
Prescriptiveness
Public doctrine may constrain; it must not simulate private execution.

Permitted public outputs

  • Doctrinal definitions
  • Declared boundaries and exclusions
  • Source precedence and conflict rules
  • Response legitimacy and legitimate non-response rules
